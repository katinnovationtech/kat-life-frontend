FAQ - OSTAYA™ Wellness Shorts

Find answers about product use, safety, technology, and purchasing.

The Products

How does OSTAYA™ work?

OSTAYA™ delivers a very low-intensity electrical stimulation around the hips and lower back, around the core area, promoting circulation and skeletal harmony over time. It's a gentle, non-invasive, and seamlessly integrated into your daily wellness routine.

Is it a medical device?

No, it is a wellness device. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult your healthcare provider before making changes to your health routine.

Usage

Can I wear it while exercising or walking?

Yes, OSTAYA™ is designed to fit seamlessly to your daily activities like walking, exercising, gardening, or even while resting.

Are there any contraindications to wearing it every day?

OSTAYA™ is safe for daily use. For best results, use it alongside regular physical activity and a balanced diet.

Comfort & Design

Are the electrodes visible?

No, the electrodes are fully integrated into the fabric, no visible wires, no bulky accessories.

Is there a visual indicator when it is active?

Yes, a subtle LED light lets you know the device is active.

Benefits

How long before I feel results?

Results vary from person to person, yet many users notice subtle improvements within the first few weeks, such as feeling more balanced, grounded, and energized. Consistent use, along with exercise and a healthy diet, is recommended for best results.

Does OSTAYA™ replace medication or supplements?

No, OSTAYA™ is a complementary wellness device and does not replace medications, supplements, or medical treatments. Always consult your doctor for medical decisions.

Technology & Safety

Do I need an app to use it?

No, you don't need an app to use OSTAYA™—just press the ON/OFF button and you're ready. If you want more control, it's also Bluetooth-compatible and can be used with the OSTAYA™ app to adjust intensity and personalize your experience.

Is the electrical stimulation painful?

No, OSTAYA™ uses a very gentle, low-intensity stimulation that's comfortable and non-invasive. The electrical stimulation does not cause any muscle contraction, nerve stimulation, or vibration.

Purchase & Access

Where can I buy OSTAYA™?

OSTAYA™ will be available directly online (DTC — direct-to-consumer), through Wareologie.com and OSTAYA™ digital channels.

Is OSTAYA™ approved by Health Canada or the FDA?

OSTAYA™ is currently positioned as a wellness device.
