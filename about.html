ABOUT KAT LIFE

Our Story

At KAT Life, we believe that feeling strong, balanced, and free to move should never fade with time. Inspired by this belief, we created OSTAYA™, a pair of smart shorts that are a wellness innovation designed to fit seamlessly into your daily life. Our goal is simple: to naturally help older men and women feel centered, upright, and connected to the movements that make everyday life yours.

Our Mission

We call our approach Lifestyle First Wellness. That means no complicated routines or medical complexity and instead just a natural addition to the life you already live. Whether you're reading, stretching, watching tv, gardening, or simply taking a walk, OSTAYA™ was made to move with you.

About OSTAYA™

OSTAYA™ is a smart, wearable short designed to promote bone vitality and postural alignment using gentle, non-invasive electrical stimulation. Seamlessly integrating into daily routines, it acts as a wellness supplement for your core.
